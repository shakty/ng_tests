Welcome to the Art Exhibition Game!

You just joined a community of artists who decided to prepare 3 (three) artistic exhibitions.

Your task as a player is to create 1 (one) work of art, and to select the best among the ones produced at each round. Each player is therefore at the same time an artist and an art critic.

The game is divided in three steps:

  1. Produce a work of art
  2. Submit it to one of three exhibitions (named A, B, or C)
  3. Review the paintings produced by other artists
The three steps above are repeated for 50 (fifty) rounds, and at the end of each you will be shown the works of art put on show on the three exhibitions.

For participating in the game you will rewarded with 0.75 CHF each time one of your works is exposed in one the three exhibitions.

You wil now play a test round during which you will be guided through the three steps of the game and you learn to use the graphical interface to produce your painintgs.
